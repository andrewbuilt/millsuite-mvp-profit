## Spreadsheets vs MillSuite Comparison

### Leads & Estimating
- Spreadsheets: "Copy last job's spreadsheet. Guess. Hope."
- MillSuite: "AI-powered estimates from historical data. Drag-drop PDF parsing. 97-99% accuracy."

### Scheduling
- Spreadsheets: "Hey did anyone tell finish dept about the schedule change?"
- MillSuite: "Visual monthly and weekly calendar by department. Drag to reschedule. Capacity at a glance."

### Time Tracking
- Spreadsheets: "Paper timesheets. Or that app nobody remembers to use. Enter hours into spreadsheet on Friday."
- MillSuite: "Built-in timers. Team members clock in on their phone. Hours roll up to projects automatically."

### Job Costing
- Spreadsheets: "Find out you lost money three weeks after the install. Wonder which job it was."
- MillSuite: "Real-time P&L per project. See margin erosion before it's too late. Alerts when hours run over."

### Material Costs
- Spreadsheets: "Save invoice PDF somewhere. Manually enter numbers. Forget to update the master sheet."
- MillSuite: "Drag-drop invoices. AI parses line items. Costs update in real time across all active jobs."

### When Someone Quits
- Spreadsheets: "Three weeks of 'where's the file for...' and 'how did they used to...'"
- MillSuite: "Everything's in the system. New person logs in day one. All history, all context, all processes."

### Pricing Future Work
- Spreadsheets: "I think the last kitchen like this was around... 150? Maybe 160 hours?"
- MillSuite: "AI learns from every completed job. Variance analysis feeds the next estimate automatically."
